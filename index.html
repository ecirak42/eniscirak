You are not broken

Agoraphobia can make you feel trapped in a life that keeps getting smaller. You may look fine on the outside while inside you’re scanning for exits, bracing for symptoms, and trying to stay one step ahead of the next panic attack. That is exhausting. It can start to feel like you’re not really living anymore. You’re just managing, avoiding, enduring. But this is not the end of your life.

The real problem is the feedback loop.

One little sensation turns into a thought: “What if this becomes a panic attack?” That thought creates more fear. The fear releases adrenaline, which makes the sensations more intense. And suddenly you’re caught in a loop. When you understand that loop, everything starts to make more sense. And when you learn how to interrupt it, everything can begin to change.

Five things you will learn while working with me:

  • what’s happening in your mind and body during panic
  • how to stop treating adrenaline as a threat and break the feedback loop
  • how to release pent up emotions and stuck energy
  • how to trust your own body again and feel safe anywhere you go
  • how to feel confident and free
